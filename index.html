Who We Are

Welcome to Our Event Management Company (Since its founding in 2003)

At Global Logistics Services, we are more than just a logistics provider — we are your reliable partner in managing the flow of goods and services efficiently across the globe. Headquartered in Hyderabad, Telangana, India, our mission is to simplify complex supply chains and deliver solutions that move your business forward. With over two decades of experience, our strength lies in precision, reliability, and customer-focused service.
